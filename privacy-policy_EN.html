2ⁿ Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 17, 2024

1. Introduction

"2ⁿ" (hereinafter referred to as "the App") is provided by an individual developer. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and disclosed in relation to the use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

The App does not collect personal information, but we may collect the following information:

  • Scores achieved by users
  • Device type and language settings

3. How We Use Information

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving App functionality and performance
  • Enhancing user experience
  • Analyzing anonymized statistical data

4. Information Sharing

The developer does not share raw collected data with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • When required to comply with legal requests
  • When necessary to protect the developer's rights

5. Data Storage and Management

The App uses Google Firebase (hereinafter "Firebase") for storing and managing collected data. Firebase is a secure cloud-based platform provided by Google. Please note the following:

  • Data is stored on Firebase servers and is encrypted.
  • We prevent unauthorized access to data in accordance with Firebase's security measures.
  • The data storage location depends on Firebase server locations, which may be outside your country.
  • Firebase's Privacy Policy and Google's Data Processing Terms apply.

While the use of Firebase allows us to provide a more secure and efficient service, please understand that Google is involved in data management.

6. Data Security

The developer implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the collected information, including the use of Firebase's security features. Data is anonymized and stored in an encrypted state. However, as no method of data transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Disclaimer

Please note that the developer and operator cannot be held responsible for any damages or disadvantages resulting from the use of this App.

8. Advertising

The App displays advertisements. We use Google AdMob for ad delivery, which may use anonymous advertising IDs. The collection and use of data related to advertising follows Google's advertising policies.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

The developer may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, we will notify you through the App and provide the latest information on this page. In case of significant changes, we may seek your consent again.
